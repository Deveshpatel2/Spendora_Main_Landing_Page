Summary of Key Points

We process personal information depending on how you interact with Spendora, the choices you make, and the features you use. We may process sensitive information, including financial data, when necessary with your consent or as otherwise permitted by law. We do not collect information from third parties except where you choose to use a supported social login or third-party integration.

We use information to provide, improve, and administer Spendora, communicate with you, protect against fraud and security issues, and comply with law. We do not sell your personal information. You may have rights to access, correct, delete, export, or restrict certain uses of your personal information depending on where you live.

Spendora does not sell your personal information or financial data. Spendora does not currently connect to your bank account or pull transaction data from financial institutions. Financial information you enter is used to provide budgeting, expense tracking, reporting, sharing, and related app features.

1. What Information Do We Collect?

Personal Information You Disclose to Us

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you register for Spendora, join our waitlist, express interest in our products or services, participate in activities on Spendora, or otherwise contact us.

The personal information we collect may include:

  • Names.
  • Email addresses.
  • Passwords and authentication credentials.
  • Financial data you choose to enter, such as expenses, budgets, categories, groups, split expenses, recurring expenses, balances, reports, and related notes.
  • Uploaded content, such as receipts or profile images, if you choose to upload them.
  • Device, browser, app version, error log, and basic usage information needed to operate and improve Spendora.

All personal information you provide must be true, complete, and accurate, and you must notify us of any changes.

Financial Data

Spendora is designed to help you organize information about your money. We use financial data you enter to power core app features such as budgeting, expense tracking, charts, summaries, group expense sharing, recurring expense reminders, and reports. We do not use your financial data to determine credit eligibility, provide loans, provide investment advice, or sell financial products.

Spendora does not currently connect to bank accounts, credit cards, brokerage accounts, or other financial institutions. If we add bank connections or similar integrations in the future, we will update this Privacy Notice and provide any additional disclosures or consent flows required by law and platform rules.

Sensitive Information

When necessary, with your consent or as otherwise permitted by applicable law, we may process financial data and other information that may be considered sensitive in certain jurisdictions.

Application Data

If you use the Spendora application, we may request permission to send push notifications about your account or app features. You can opt out of push notifications in your device settings. Application data is primarily needed to maintain security, operate the app, troubleshoot issues, and support internal analytics and reporting.

Google API

If Spendora uses information received from Google APIs, our use and transfer of that information will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

10. Controls for Do-Not-Track Features

Most web browsers and some mobile operating systems include a Do-Not-Track ("DNT") setting. Because there is no uniform technology standard for recognizing and implementing DNT signals, we do not currently respond to DNT browser signals or other mechanisms that automatically communicate a choice not to be tracked online. If a standard is adopted that we must follow in the future, we will describe that practice in a revised version of this Privacy Notice.
