Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Spendora collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our website, join our waitlist, or use the Spendora mobile app.

Spendora is designed to help you manage personal finances. We are not a bank, credit provider, broker, tax advisor, or financial advisor.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use Spendora, we may collect:

  • Contact information, such as your email address when you join the waitlist or contact us.
  • Account information, such as your name, email address, password credentials, preferred currency, and profile settings.
  • Finance information you choose to enter, such as expenses, budgets, categories, groups, split expenses, recurring expenses, and related notes.
  • Uploaded content, such as receipts or profile images if you choose to upload them.
  • Technical information, such as device type, browser type, app version, IP address, error logs, and basic usage activity.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide, operate, secure, and improve Spendora.
  • Create and manage accounts, budgets, expenses, groups, reports, and app preferences.
  • Send waitlist, launch, account, verification, password reset, and support messages.
  • Detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, abuse, security incidents, and technical issues.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers who help us run Spendora, such as hosting, database, analytics, email delivery, authentication, file storage, and customer support providers. We may also share information when required by law, to protect rights and safety, or in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets.

Data Storage and Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect information. No online service can guarantee absolute security, so you should use a strong password, keep your login credentials private, and contact us if you suspect unauthorized access.

Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide Spendora, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and maintain security. Waitlist emails may be kept until you unsubscribe or ask us to delete them.

Your Choices and Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or export of your personal information by contacting us. You may also opt out of marketing emails by using the unsubscribe link when available or by contacting us directly. Depending on where you live, you may have additional privacy rights under laws such as the California Consumer Privacy Act or similar state privacy laws.

Children's Privacy

Spendora is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us so we can delete it.

International Users

If you use Spendora from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. These countries may have different data protection laws than your location.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are material, we will take reasonable steps to notify users, such as updating the effective date or providing an in-app or email notice.

Contact Us

For privacy requests or questions, contact us at supportspendora@gmail.com.
