Delete Your Spendora Data

Last updated: May 12, 2026

You can delete some Spendora data without deleting your account. This page explains the steps for deleting data in the Spendora app or requesting deletion from our support team.

Public data deletion URL: https://spendorapp.com/delete-data.

Delete Data in the App

You can delete the following data directly in the Spendora app where those features are available:

  • Expenses.
  • Budgets.
  • Recurring expenses.
  • Split groups.
  • Group expenses.
  • Splits and settlements.

Request Deletion of Other Data

To request deletion of other Spendora data without deleting your account, email support@spendorapp.com with the subject line Data Deletion Request. Please include the email address associated with your Spendora account and a description of the data you want deleted.

Data We May Keep

Some data may be retained if required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, dispute resolution, backup recovery, or enforcement of our Terms of Service. Backup copies may remain for a limited period before being overwritten or deleted according to our retention practices. Any retained information will be handled according to our Privacy Policy.

Delete Your Entire Account

To delete your full Spendora account and associated app data, visit Delete Your Account.
