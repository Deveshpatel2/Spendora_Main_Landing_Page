Delete Your Spendora Account

Last updated: May 12, 2026

You can delete your Spendora account directly in the app by going to Settings > Delete Account.

Public account deletion URL: https://spendorapp.com/delete-account.

Request Deletion by Email

If you cannot access the app or need help deleting your account, contact us at supportspendora@gmail.com. We may need to verify that you own the account before deleting it.

What Gets Deleted

Account deletion removes your Spendora account and associated app data, including:

  • Account information.
  • Expenses.
  • Budgets.
  • Groups.
  • Splits.
  • Settlements.
  • Recurring expenses.
  • Refresh tokens.
  • Push notification tokens.

Limited Retention

We may retain limited information if required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, dispute resolution, or enforcement of our Terms of Service. Any retained information will be handled according to our Privacy Policy.
